Welcome to Utang Tracker Just Now
Thank you for visiting! This is Utang Tracker, a simple debt tracking app.

Notes:
  • Important Notice: Your debt data is stored locally in your browser. Please make a note of your debts to ensure you don't lose your data if you clear your browser's history or switch devices.

  • Important Notice: The data in this application does not have a backend feature and database and it is strictly exclusive to your own devices only. You can't have the same list on multiple devices
